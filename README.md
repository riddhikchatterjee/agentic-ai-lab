# Agentic AI Lab

A practical engineering journey into Agentic AI through iterative development of AI agents, progressing from simple assistants to enterprise-grade multi-agent systems.

## Vision

Build 1000 AI agents while learning Agentic AI from first principles through hands-on development.

The objective is not only to learn AI concepts, but also to develop production-grade engineering practices around AI systems.
